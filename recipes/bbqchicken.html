BBQ Chicken

Easiest BBQ Chicken Recipe

I am going to show you the easiest and delicious BBQ Chicken Recipe, that you can make for your family or any backyard grilling.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup lemon-lime soda (such as 7-Up®)
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • 4 cloves crushed garlic
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 medium lemon, juiced
  • 2 ½ pounds skinless, boneless chicken thighs, cut in half

Steps

  1. Add lemon-lime soda, soy sauce, garlic, brown sugar, black pepper, and lemon juice for the marinade. Whisk until combined. Add halved chicken thighs and toss to coat.
  2. Cover with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator for 4 to 18 hours.
  3. When you are ready to cook the chicken, combine reserved 3 tablespoons banana ketchup, soy sauce, brown sugar, oil, and fish sauce in a small bowl for basting sauce.
  4. Preheat a charcoal grill until coals are very hot.
  5. Remove chicken from the refrigerator and place on metal skewers, folding in half with the smooth side on the outside, the rougher side folded in.
  6. Grill chicken over hot coals until no longer pink in the centers, 15 to 20 minutes, turning and basting every 2 to 3 minutes. Serve with accumulated juices or extra basting sauce.
